ABOUT

A fitness club is a place that houses exercises equipment for the purpose of physical exercise. Alan Smithee describes a fitness club as a “company providing people, access to controlled environment condition space and services focused on physical fitness and health in exchange of a fee.”

Trainings

There are many trainers who are highly qualified for teaching. Here we train beginers with the best equipments and the best guidance from the trainers. Even though with best equipments but without best guidance you cannot move forward. So we provide both the best trainer and equipments so that you can have the best experience.

Workout Area

There is a large room for workout area with all the latest equipments that will give you the best workout for your body. Also we have certified teachers for beginners that can guide you through the processes.

Cardio Area

There is also a room for cardio theater with lots of cardiovascular training related equipment such as rowing machines, stationary exercise bikes, elliptical trainers and treadmills. These includes a number of audio-visual displays, often TVs.

Group Exercise Class

These includes aerobics, cycling (spinning), boxing or martial arts, high intensity training, yoga, pilates, muscle training, stretching. The instructors are certified with more than 5 years of experience.

Sports Facilities

There are sports facilities like swimming pools, squash courts, indoor running tracks and boxing area.
